> What is RoadToTrading?_

RoadToTrading (RTT) is a cutting-edge web application designed to empower traders with the power of artificial intelligence. Our mission is to demystify the complexities of technical analysis, making it accessible and actionable for everyone, from novice investors to seasoned professionals.

Using the advanced capabilities of Google's Gemini model, RTT analyzes candlestick chart images to identify key trading patterns. It provides clear, concise, and data-driven insights, including potential buy/sell signals, profit targets, and stop-loss levels, helping you make more informed trading decisions.

> Our Vision_

We believe that the future of trading lies in the synergy between human intuition and machine intelligence. RTT is more than just a tool; it's your personal trading analyst, working 24/7 to find opportunities in the market. We are committed to continuous improvement, constantly refining our algorithms and expanding our features to stay ahead of the curve.
